About me

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineering MEng student, currently studying at the University of Bristol. I am mainly interested in projects involving communications, signal processing, machine learning, back-end as well as front-end development, and I always look forward for ways to expand my knowledge and explore new ideas to deliver a final product. Each one of my projects started out as a new learning experience with a clear motivation and goal in mind and I regularly update them in order to implement additional features and improvements. Because of this, I am always available for new project ideas and opportunities. Further information about me and my projects can be found in my resume as well as my project portfolio.

Geometric Shape Analyser

An application used to analyse a set of points on the plane and identify different geometric shapes, given their mathematical properties. Offline Encrypted Database

An application used to safely store sensitive data locally, behind a login system. It features a user-friendly interface, allowing the user to organise their data by registering and managing multiple accounts. Password verification is achieved with sha256 and an encryption algorithm is used to read and store user data in a spreadsheet database.
